Sudanese Social Volunteer

Sudan faces the world's worst humanitarian crisis: 37.8 million people need assistance. Since April 2023, over 10 million have been displaced. We're delivering emergency medical care, education, legal aid, and community support across 12 displacement camps and 82 localities. Every euro saves lives.

Registered & Accredited

SSV is a registered nonprofit organization (Gemeinnützige Organisation) in Germany. Certified by the German Federal Office for Migration and Refugees (BAMF). Transparent International operates with full financial accountability. Annual audits published publicly.
